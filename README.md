# QRXL

A QR code generator that lives in one HTML file. Drop it anywhere, it just works.

## What it does

Paste a URL (or any text), hit generate, get a QR code. Download it as PNG or SVG, copy it to your clipboard, or pick custom colors if you want it to match something.

That's it.

## Why I made this

Every QR generator online is either covered in ads, wants you to sign up, or makes you pay to download the PNG. This one doesn't do any of that. No account, no tracking, nothing leaves your browser.

## Features

- Custom colors for dark and light modules
- Sizes from 128px up to 1024px (big enough for print)
- PNG + SVG download
- Copy to clipboard
- Saves your last 10 codes locally so you don't have to retype stuff

## Stack

Plain HTML, CSS, and vanilla JS. Uses [qrcodejs](https://github.com/davidshimjs/qrcodejs) loaded from a CDN. That's the only dependency and it's tiny.
